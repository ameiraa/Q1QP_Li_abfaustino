Women's Universal Right to Vote

The FDT shows that out of 52 countries, 48 (92.3%) give women the right to vote while 4 (7.69%) countries do not. This will be used as the basis for the bar graph and pie chart.

The bar graph illustrates the number of countries and women’s right to vote in them. 4 countries do not give women the right to vote, while 48 do. The x axis is the right to vote (yes or no) and the y axis is the number of countries.

The pie chart shows the percentage of women’s universal right to vote (yes or no) in all the countries part of this research. Only 7.7% (4 countries) do not allow women to vote, while the rest of the 92.3% (48 countries) do.
