Women's Access to the Justice System

The table presents the number of countries with access and the number of countries with limited access to the justice system along with relative frequency. Overall, a larger percentage (63.46%) of countries give women full access to the justice system, an effect of the consistent efforts of the women rights movement.

The bar graph illustrates the number of countries that have access and limited access to the justice system. The x axis is whether the country has access or limited access to the justice system. The y axis is the number of countries. One can see that more countries give women full access to the justice system than countries that don’t.

The pie chart illustrates the percentage of countries with access to the justice system (the percentages are rounded off). While the portion of countries with women that have full access are larger than the portion with limited access, there is around ⅖ of the sample that limit women’s access to the justice system.
