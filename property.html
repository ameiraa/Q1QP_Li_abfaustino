Women's Property Rights

The table shows how many countries grant women property rights. As we can see women do have property rights, but there are just some hindrances for some countries. But overall for most countries, women have the capacity to hold sole ownership.

Putting all the data in a bar graph can help us visualize more how there are only a few countries that hinder women from having full property rights. It's good to see that the bar labeled “Yes, women have the capacity to hold sole ownership” is the tallest one compared to the other 2 bars.

LEGEND (because it got cut off)

Blue - Yes, women have the capacity to hold sole ownership.

Yellow - Yes, but typically contingent on a formal association with a male owner.

Red - Yes, women have the capacity to hold sole ownership, however its traditions hinder it.


Here, we can visualize that more than 75% of countries allow women to have sole ownership (84.6%). Only 15% of the countries hinder women from having full property rights.
